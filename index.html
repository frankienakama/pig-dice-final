Pig Dice

How to Play:

  1. During your turn, click roll!
  2. If you roll a 1, your turn ends and you gain zero points.
  3. If you roll any other number, it gets added to the turn total.
  4. If you choose to hold, then the turn total is added to your game total and it is then the other player's turn.
  5. First person to 100 wins!
